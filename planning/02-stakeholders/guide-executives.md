# Interview Guide — Executives (CEO / CFO / CRO / CBO)
**Stakeholder profile (source §3):** goals — profitable growth within risk appetite; strategic differentiation; no headline risk. Concerns — reputational damage from "AI decides your credit" stories; investment without measurable return; internal conflict between Risk and Business. Their success metrics — risk-adjusted revenue uplift; NPS movement; program cost vs. quantified opportunity.
**Note:** Finance has no separate row in the §3 map (OQ-14) — CFO questions below carry the Finance-owner duties for D5/D9/H6; confirm a named Finance delegate in-session.
**Session:** 45–60 min each or one joint session (week 3); separate CRO time recommended for appetite specifics.

## Objectives
1. Fix the decision economics: E1's materiality threshold and value floor, and the investment appetite behind E3 (OQ-13).
2. Confirm risk appetite headroom with the CRO office (A5; OQ-06).
3. Test — explicitly — acceptance of the no-go principle (§10) and of pivot outcomes.
4. Resolve the deadlock question: who arbitrates the Risk-vs-Business shared metric if E7 stalls (charter escalation).

## Questions (11)
1. Why this initiative, why now — and what alternative uses of the same investment did it beat? *(strategic intent; E3 context)*
2. What is your risk appetite headroom today — capital, provisioning, concentration? *(Appendix B-6, verbatim — CRO seat; A5: "If the bank is at its capital/appetite ceiling, no optimization is permissible")*
3. What opportunity size — risk-adjusted, net — makes this worth pursuing, and below what floor should we recommend stopping? *(OQ-13; fixes E1/E3 bars* before *evidence lands)*
4. The charter states a no-go recommendation is a successful outcome of this phase. Do you agree, and will you say so to the team? *(§10 principle; R-BUS-05 sunk-cost inoculation — get it minuted)*
5. If the diagnosis says "the model is fine — invest in offer policy instead," what would you do with that answer? *(§10's named pivot; tests P1-attachment at the top)*
6. Who would be accountable if an "optimized" offer defaults? *(Appendix B-9, verbatim; OQ-09; D10)*
7. When Risk and Business deadlock on the shared metric, who arbitrates, and by what principle? *(E7 escalation path — name the person now)*
8. How much "AI adjusts your credit offer" headline risk will you carry for how much upside — and what framing would you never allow? *(§3 concern; H10 concept constraints; communications boundary for D11)*
9. What evidence do you have that competitors are poaching under-served customers? *(§1.3; H11/OPT2 context)*
10. Which executive owns this program as Sponsor, and what decision rights do they hold vs. the steering committee? *(OQ-23; charter §4)*
11. What must the week-10 steering pack contain for you to make a clean proceed/pivot/stop decision in one sitting? *(E10 design input; steering-pack outline validation)*

## Evidence to collect
- Risk appetite statement + current headroom view (M7, with CRO office).
- Strategy documents referencing the initiative; any board material that pre-commits outcomes (sunk-cost risk flag).
- Named: Sponsor, Finance delegate, E7 arbiter, E1/E3 thresholds (or the process and date for setting them).

## Tensions to probe
- **Risk-vs-Business conflict (their §3 concern, primary):** do they see the conflict as a people problem or a metric problem? The source's answer is a shared risk-adjusted metric (E7) — will the CEO/CFO enforce joint signature rather than side with a camp?
- **Growth story vs. discovery honesty:** is there appetite for ranges-with-downside (E3 requires a downside scenario), or pressure for a single big number (R-BUS-01)?
- **No-go sincerity:** watch for "yes, but obviously we'll proceed" — that response is itself a risk-register trigger (R-BUS-05).

## Feed into D3 (and beyond)
- Position summaries per executive; decision-economics record (thresholds, floors, arbiter, sponsor) → charter, E1/E3 gates, open-questions closures (OQ-06, OQ-09, OQ-13, OQ-14, OQ-23).
- Tension-log entries with attribution (which executive holds which line) — steering-pack pre-wire map.
