# D3 — Stakeholder Analysis & Engagement Log (template)
**Source §9:** "interviews completed, positions, tensions, RACI proposal for the future layer" · **Owner:** Product · **Sign-off:** Program sponsor · **Supports gates:** E7/E8 climate; KPI §8.1 (100% group coverage incl. front line and Model Risk)

## 1. Coverage scorecard
> Guidance: table of all 11 mapped groups (Customer, Credit Risk, Model Risk/Validation, Business/Commercial, Compliance/Legal, Data Science, Product, Executives, Customer Service/Front line, Internal Audit, Regulator-indirect) × sessions held / planned / declined. KPI target: 100% of groups. Regulator row records the H13 dialogue decision (held / not pursued, with reason) — direct engagement is never assumed.

## 2. Position summaries per group
> Guidance: one subsection per group: observed goals, concerns, success metrics **vs. the §3 map's predictions** — confirmed, shifted, or contradicted, with interview evidence. Keep verbatim quotes where they carry weight; attribute carefully (agreed attribution rules).
> Required evidence: interview notes referenced per the engagement log (§6 below); guides used from `../02-stakeholders/`.

## 3. Tension log
> Guidance: the working register of live tensions. Must include, at minimum, the §3 flagged tension: "Business and Risk have structurally opposed near-term metrics" and its resolution path (shared risk-adjusted metric → D9/E7). For each tension: parties, substance, evidence, severity, owner, resolution path, status. Feed risk register (R-BUS-02) where a tension threatens a gate.

## 4. Trust and readiness assessment (A9)
> Guidance: disposition A9 ("Stakeholders will trust AI-assisted offer adjustments") with evidence per group — interview signals, H12 mock-review outcome, H10 staff-side results. State plainly where trust is conditional and on what.

## 5. RACI proposal for the future layer
> Guidance: a *proposal* (not a decision) for who would be R/A/C/I on the future layer's recommendations, escalations, and monitoring — consistent with D10's accountability model and OQ-09 answers. Mark explicitly as input to D10/E8, drafted for Mid Phase.

## 6. Engagement log
> Guidance: append-only session register: date, group, participants (role level), guide used, artifacts collected, follow-ups owed. This is the KPI evidence — keep it current, not reconstructed.

## 7. Open items and escalations
> Guidance: interview commitments not yet honored (data pointers, workshop dates), groups resisting engagement, OQ entries raised from sessions.
