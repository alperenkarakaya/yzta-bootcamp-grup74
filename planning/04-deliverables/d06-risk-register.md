# D6 — Risk Register (deliverable template)
**Source §9:** "business/AI/regulatory/operational/data risks with owners and Phase-2 mitigations" · **Owner:** Product + Risk · **Sign-off:** CRO office · **Supports:** E10 steering pack; every gate's failure modes

> Guidance: the living register is maintained at `../05-risk-and-compliance/risk-register.md` (all 26 §6 risks, IDs R-BUS/R-AI/R-REG/R-OPS/R-DAT). This deliverable is the reviewed, signed snapshot of that register at phase close, extended with Phase-2 mitigations per source §9.

## 1. Register snapshot
> Guidance: full register table as of the review workshop (workplan wk7): ID, category, description, likelihood, impact, owner, Early Phase mitigation status (done / partial / not done — honestly), trigger indicators observed (which fired during the phase — evidence, not embarrassment).

## 2. Risks that fired during the Early Phase
> Guidance: any risk whose trigger indicators were observed (e.g., data slips, negative legal drafts, KPI deadlock signals) — what happened, what it changed, current status. A fired risk is discovery working as intended.

## 3. Phase-2 mitigations
> Guidance: the source §9 requirement distinguishing this deliverable from the working register: for each risk surviving into Mid Phase, the mitigation Mid Phase must carry (e.g., R-AI-02 feedback loops is explicitly "a Mid Phase design requirement" per §6.2 — carried as a requirement, not designed here). Each mitigation has an owner-designate and a trigger for activation.

## 4. New risks discovered
> Guidance: risks not in §6 that discovery surfaced — added with the same ID scheme (next number in category), source-traced to the evidence that revealed them, and logged to open-questions if they imply scope questions.

## 5. Risk acceptance and escalations
> Guidance: which residual risks the CRO office accepts at phase close, which escalate to the steering committee as E10 decision inputs (e.g., E9's blocked-path residual risk requires executive acceptance).
