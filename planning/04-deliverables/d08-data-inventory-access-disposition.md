# D8 — Data Inventory & Access Disposition (template)
**Source §9:** "every category: exists / accessible / legal basis / quality / owner" · **Owner:** Data Science + Data Office · **Sign-off:** CDO or equivalent · **Feeds gates:** E5 (primary), evidences A7

> Guidance: this deliverable is the signed, complete state of `../03-data/data-inventory-tracker.md`. The tracker is the working document; D8 is its dispositioned snapshot. The §7 access rule is the frame: "Access is **not assumed**."

## 1. Disposition summary
> Guidance: headline table per tier — counts of AVAILABLE-USABLE / CONDITIONAL / BLOCKED / DO-NOT-COLLECT. The E5 test: "100% of §7.1 categories marked available-and-usable, or an approved workaround exists per gap." KPI §8.1: "Mandatory data categories confirmed available + legally usable — 100% dispositioned."

## 2. Mandatory categories M1–M9 — full disposition
> Required evidence per item: exists? / accessible? (environment + path) / legal basis (confirmed by Compliance) / quality assessment (profiling summary, known defects, definition changes mid-history) / owner (named) / blocking issue / disposition. OQ-19 (24–36 months of M4) answered explicitly.

## 3. Gaps and approved workarounds
> Guidance: one entry per Mandatory gap: the gap, the proposed workaround, who approved it, and what analytical claims the workaround weakens (honesty requirement — a workaround that silently degrades H1's evidence quality must say so, feeding D4 caveats). Unapproved gaps trigger E5's consequence: "Phase extended; Mid Phase not started on hoped-for data."

## 4. Useful and Optional categories — disposition
> Guidance: U1–U8 with the same columns; hypothesis linkage stated per item (§7 minimization rule). OPT1/OPT4 recorded as DO-NOT-COLLECT with the rule citation (OQ-22); OPT5 disposition records the fairness-audit legal gate outcome (approved protocol / blocked), cross-referenced to D7 §5.

## 5. Quality profile summaries
> Required evidence: profiling results for delivered items (coverage, gaps, consistency across systems — §6.5 data-quality risk), with implications for which hypotheses were testable at what confidence (feeds D4 §6 lineage).

## 6. Legal-basis register
> Guidance: the completed mandatory legal-basis column (§6.5: "Legal basis column mandatory in the inventory"), with purpose-limitation notes for repurposed data and bureau-license findings (OQ-24) — co-owned with D7 §6.

## 7. Access governance record
> Guidance: environments used, who had access to what, pseudonymization applied, retention/deletion commitments per the data request pack §2 — the audit trail of the diagnostic itself (Internal Audit expectation).
