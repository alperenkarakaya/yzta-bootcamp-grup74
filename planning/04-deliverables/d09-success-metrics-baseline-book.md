Both signatures on this page.

## 2. Product KPI definitions and baselines (§8.2 — all eight)
> Guidance: one subsection per source-listed KPI, each with: definition, formula and formula owner, measurement source (tracker item), Phase-1 measured baseline, and the §8.2 definition discipline satisfied:
> 1. **Reduction in under-classification rate** — requires the E2 ground-truth definition (A2); baseline measured on historical data.
> 2. **Approved credit volume uplift (risk-adjusted)** — net of expected loss, capital, funding; formula agreed with Finance & Risk (H6 method).
> 3. **Portfolio risk stability** — default/delinquency of touched cohorts within the pre-agreed tolerance band vs. control expectation.
> 4. **Customer satisfaction (NPS/CSAT on credit journey)** — credit-journey-specific baseline (U5), not bank-wide NPS.
> 5. **Revenue uplift per optimized customer** — attribution method agreed *before any pilot exists* (source verbatim).
> 6. **Complaint rate on credit limits/offers** — baseline from the H9 complaint-taxonomy work (M8).
> 7. **Fairness indicators** — disparity metrics chosen with Compliance; baseline measured (H14), or the blocked-path note cross-referenced to D7 §5.
> 8. **Explainability/trust indicators** — staff and customer trust measures piloted qualitatively in Phase 1 (H10 results).

## 3. Baseline evidence register
> Required evidence: per KPI — data extract reference, measurement date, method note, and the analysis file it came from (H6/H9/H10/H14 outputs; tracker lineage). Baselines without evidence pointers do not count.

## 4. Phase-1 KPI scorecard (§8.1)
