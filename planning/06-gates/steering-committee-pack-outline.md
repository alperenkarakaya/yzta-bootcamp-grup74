# Steering Committee Pack — Outline (week 10, E10 session)
**Purpose:** enable one clean, minuted decision: **proceed / pivot / stop**. The pack is built so all three recommendations are decidable from the same evidence — per the §10 charter principle, a pack engineered to permit only "proceed" is a defective pack.
**Discipline:** pre-read issued ≥ 3 working days before the session (pre-wired with CRO office and Executives in week 9); session time spent on the decision, not on discovery of the material.

## 1. Decision asked (1 page)
The E10 question verbatim: proceed to Mid Phase with charter D11, pivot scope, or stop — with rationale to be minuted. State that no-go is a valid outcome, quoting the charter clause. Name the three options with equal typography.

## 2. Phase KPI scorecard (1 page)
The eight §8.1 KPIs with final status (from D9 §4): assumptions dispositioned (target 100%), hypotheses tested/scheduled (target ≥10 tested), P-mix quantified with Risk sign-off, sizing board-presentable, mandatory data 100% dispositioned, interview coverage 100% incl. front line and Model Risk, written legal opinion delivered, shared metric signed.

## 3. The diagnosis (2–3 pages, from D1)
Quantified P1/P2/P3 mix with ranges; which sub-problem dominates and what that means for the concept (§1.1: the value proposition "differs materially"); root-cause dispositions; the caveats box (selective labels, counterfactual limits) — visible, not footnoted.

## 4. Evidence digest (2 pages, from D4)
A1–A10 dispositions; H1–H14 outcomes against their verbatim falsification conditions; falsified hypotheses presented with equal prominence; "scheduled" hypotheses with their data dependencies and dates.

## 5. Opportunity ranking and value case (2–3 pages, from D5)
Ranked O1–O8: net risk-adjusted ranges, downside scenario, governance friction (§4 ratings), legal viability flags (E4), per-segment win-win results (A10), affordability exclusions. MVP candidate scope — scope only, no design. If nothing clears the value floor: that finding, plainly.

## 6. Regulatory and fairness position (1–2 pages, from D7)
Classification opinion summary (H13) and per-opportunity viability; jurisdiction scan highlights (incl. AI-specific regulation applicability); fairness position (H14): findings or the formally-blocked record with executive acceptance (E9).

## 7. Data disposition (1 page, from D8)
Mandatory tier disposition (E5), approved workarounds and what they weaken, blocked items with consequences for later phases.

## 8. Shared metric and baselines (1 page, from D9)
The signed one-pager reproduced; tolerance band; baseline table (§8.2 KPIs) — the later-phase scoreboard both sides already signed (E7).

## 9. Governance readiness (1 page, from D10 + D2)
Accountability model accepted in principle by Model Risk + Audit (E8) with their conditions; insertion feasibility summary (E6): viable vendor-compliant points found or not; downstream-dependency warnings (R-BUS-04).

## 10. Risk position (1 page, from D6)
Top risks by exposure; triggers that fired during the phase and what they changed; Phase-2 mitigations that condition any proceed decision; residual risks requiring acceptance in this session.

## 11. Gate status board (1 page)
E1–E9 statuses from `exit-criteria-checklist.md` — met / not met / met-with-conditions — each with its consequence if not met, so the committee sees what any override would actually mean.

## 12. The three recommendations, argued (2 pages)
- **PROCEED:** the D11 draft charter (scope, boundaries — "the layer shall never…" quoted verbatim — timeline, resources, conditions precedent).
- **PIVOT:** the strongest alternative supported by evidence (e.g., offer-policy recalibration through Risk via the O7 route; measurement-only O5 scope; §10's "the model is fine, invest in offer policy instead"), with its own scope sketch and what it preserves.
- **STOP:** the case, plus the preserved-value statement — what the bank keeps regardless: the D1 diagnosis, the D9 baseline book, the O5 under-lending metric concept, the D2 current-state map, the D7 regulatory position.
> Drafting note: the working group may mark one recommendation as recommended, but all three are argued from the evidence — the committee decides (E10).

## 13. Decision and minutes
The E10 minuted-decision template (from `exit-criteria-checklist.md`) executed live: gate record, options minuted, decision, rationale, dissents, conditions, signatures. "Phase is not closed until a decision is minuted."

## Appendices
Full D1–D11 as pre-read attachments; open-questions log closure status; engagement log summary (D3).
